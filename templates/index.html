Why Does This Service Required?

Over 2 Million children under the age of 5 die every year in India* and another 1 Million or more are disabled for life. A major cause is that parents often forget to vaccinate the child on time.Hence here we provide vaccine reminder so parents can remind before every due shot !!!

Vaccination reminder services in several countries have been effective in increasing compliance by 20%. There are over 40 published scientific studies in the Cochrane database that prove the effectiveness of vaccination reminders.
